WELCOME

Welcome to Dublin Georgia "The Warrior's way training center". We introduce arts and styles that are not found commonly anywhere else around Laurens County. Learn arts from Karate Kenpo, Bujinkan Ninjutsu, and Japanese jujutsu. Come out and try some classes yourself.

About

We are the "The Warrior's Way Training Center" of Dublin Ga. We train in styles of martial arts that no other school in Dublin trains in. Styles that everyone thought they knew from reading manga or watching movies and anime on television.
No we are the real deal teaching the real art of Bujinkan Ninjutsu, Jujutsu and Kenpo karate. No other school here in Dublin will teach you moves to properly defend yourself as well as love ones better than "The Warrior's Way Training Center". So come out and try some classes enrich yourselves in a rich history of ancient historic martial arts vaguely unheard of in regions such as the south. Education, motivation, and teamwork...Martial arts that we teach aren't just about the offensive nature or the defensive nature...its about a historic way of life and living.

We train anyone who wants to learn

offering self-defense classes for adults and kids in: JUJUTSU KARATE KENPO BUJINKAN NINJUTSU
